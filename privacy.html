Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

BEU ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a skincare routine tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use BEU. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the App, including users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions with data protection laws.

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, BEU, operated by an independent developer, is the data controller responsible for the processing of personal data described in this policy. For data protection inquiries, contact us at support@beu.app.

2. Information We Collect

a) Authentication Data

When you sign in using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive the following information from your identity provider:

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo URL

This data is processed through Firebase Authentication and is used solely to identify your account.

b) User-Generated Content

  • Profile photo: If you upload a custom profile photo using your camera or photo library, this image is uploaded to Firebase Storage (Google LLC) under your authenticated user account for cross-device access.
  • Routine data: All skincare routines, steps, products, and completion tracking data you create within the App are synced to Firebase Firestore (Google LLC) under your authenticated user account for cross-device access and backup. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Google.

c) Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized usage events (e.g., feature usage, onboarding progress) through Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) to understand how the App is used and to improve it. These events are linked to your user ID but do not include personal content such as routine names, product names, or skincare data.

d) Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not intentionally collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data through the App
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to the App
  • To display your name and profile photo within the App
  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App (via Firebase Analytics)

We do NOT use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing purposes
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Profiling or automated decision-making

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: Processing your authentication data is necessary to provide you with the App's services, as agreed when you create an account.
  • Legitimate Interest: We have a legitimate interest in maintaining the security and proper functioning of the App, and in collecting anonymized usage analytics to improve the App.
  • Consent: Where required by law, we obtain your consent before processing your data. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at support@beu.app.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Authentication data: Managed by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC), stored on secure servers operated by Google. Firebase Authentication data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • User-generated content (routines, products, completion history, profile photos): Stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage (Google LLC) under your authenticated user account. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Google. You may delete this data at any time through the App's account deletion feature.
  • Local cache: The App maintains a local cache of your data on your device for offline access. This cache is encrypted by the operating system and is cleared when you uninstall the App or delete your account.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal data we process. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your authentication data may be processed by Google LLC in the United States and other jurisdictions where Google operates. Google implements appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC): Manages user sign-in and account data.
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Firestore (Google LLC): Stores user-generated content (routines, products, completion history, profile data) under your authenticated user account.
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Storage (Google LLC): Stores profile photos uploaded by the user under their authenticated user account.
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC): Collects crash reports and non-fatal errors to improve App stability. Only technical data (error details, stack traces, user ID) is collected — no personal information such as names or email addresses.
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC): Collects anonymized usage events (e.g., feature usage, onboarding progress) to help us understand how the App is used and improve it. Events are linked to your user ID but do not include personal content. No advertising identifiers are collected.
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC): Provides Google account authentication.
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign-In (Apple Inc.): Provides Apple ID authentication.
    Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties. We do not use advertising or tracking services.

7. Data Retention

  • Authentication data is retained for as long as your account remains active.
  • User-generated content (routines, products, completion history, profile photos) is stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage for as long as your account remains active. You may delete this data at any time using the account deletion feature within the App.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated authentication data at any time by contacting us at support@beu.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Object: You may object to our processing of your data based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@beu.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days. If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the App.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

We do not sell, share, or use your personal information for targeted advertising. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@beu.app.

10. Children's Privacy

BEU is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@beu.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" above and notify you through the App. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
